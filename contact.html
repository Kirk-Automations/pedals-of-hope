Volunteer With Us

We're always looking for people who want to make a difference

Help our team assemble and prepare adaptive bikes for delivery. Training provided. Mechanical experience a plus but not required.

Community Outreach

Spread the word at schools, therapy centers, and community events. Help us reach families who don't know we exist.

Partner With Us

We work with businesses, foundations, therapy centers, schools, and community organizations. Partnership opportunities include:

  • Corporate sponsorship & employee giving programs
  • Equipment manufacturer partnerships
  • Therapy center referral networks
  • Community event co-hosting
  • School & district partnerships
  • Foundation grants & funding

Contact us at pedalsofhopeorg@gmail.com to explore how we can work together.

Pedals of Hope
