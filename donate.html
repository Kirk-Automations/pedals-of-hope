Where Your Money Goes

We’re a brand-new nonprofit, so we don’t have a year of financials to point at yet. What we can promise: every donation goes toward getting an adaptive bike into the hands of a specific child. No paid staff, no fancy offices, no marketing budget.

Our first annual report will be published once we’ve completed a full year of grants. Until then, if you want to know exactly where your gift went, ask us. We’ll tell you the child’s first name, the bike model, and the date they took delivery.
