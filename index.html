Our Mission

Adaptive cycling for every ability

Muscular dystrophy, cerebral palsy, spinal muscular atrophy, spina bifida — conditions that make a standard bike impossible don’t have to mean sitting out.

Adaptive tricycles give full trunk and lateral support to riders who need extra stability. Hand cycles put the freedom of riding in the hands of children with limited or no leg function. Pedals of Hope connects families with the right equipment so no child is left watching from the sidewalk.

How It Works

Three steps to a first ride

We keep the process personal — every application is reviewed by real people.

1. Apply Online

Fill out our intake form with your child’s needs, therapist recommendations, and any medical documentation.

2. Get Matched

We work with certified adaptive equipment specialists to find the right trike, hand cycle, or add-on for your child’s abilities and goals.

3. Ride!

Your child receives a custom-fitted adaptive bike, fully funded or on a sliding scale — then the real fun begins.
